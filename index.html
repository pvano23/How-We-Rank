Purpose & Goals We For You

As mentioned in our home page, I am committed to helping you achieve your athletic goals. I am tasked in offering scouting services with detailed film evaluations using my advanced NFL-level grading scale to assess your skills. This scouting service is designed to highlight your value to coaches and help them filter through potential recruits. Once evaluated, your profile is added to my prospect board, I am proud to send it to over 175+ college coaches each week. The most important aspect for me, as a scout, not a recruiter, my goal is to amplify your exposure to coaches!

Our Criteria Scouting Report

Once you fill out the form under the category “Profile” dropdown “Players Get Scouted”, as mentioned I will receive standard information about you then depending on your chosen the criteria changes on how depth your film evaluation has been done. The prospect boards include both American and Canadian players, providing a comprehensive view of potential talent across North America.

Highlight Scouting Report

  • Player Overview:
    • Position, Class, Team, Height, Weight, GPA, Overall Grade
  • Strengths:
    • Coverage Skills
    • Tackling Ability
    • Route Running
    • Mental and Physical Attributes (Instincts, Processing Speed, Competitive Toughness)
  • Weaknesses:
    • Areas for Improvement in Physicality and Consistency
  • Development Potential:
    • Projected Fit at the Next Level
    • Suitability for Various Schemes

Full Game Scouting Report

  • Player Profile:
    • Height, Weight, Position
  • Game Analysis:
    • In-depth Performance Breakdown from 3 Full Games
    • Key Strengths: Mobility, Pass-Rushing, Length
    • Areas for Growth: Physicality, Hand Technique
  • Scheme Fit:
    • Projection in Different Defensive Schemes
    • Future Development Potential
  • Additional Information:
    • Player Contact Details
    • Access to Game Film for Further Evaluation
Scouting Grade Breakdown

This section will give you an overview of the different components of our grading system.

ELITE LEVEL PROSPECT:
PROJECTION: D1 FBS.
This player is an elite athlete with standout traits, making them a top-tier recruit that can compete at the FBS level.
SCORE:85+

HIGH LEVEL PROSPECT:
PROJECTION: D1 FCS, D2.
This player possesses high-level talent and a skill set worthy of competing at the D1 FCS level and top-tier D2 programs,
though they may still require further skill development.
SCORE:75-84

GOOD LEVEL PROSPECT:
PROJECTION: D1 FCS, D2, U SPORTS.
This player has strong measurables and athletic traits but lacks the size or skill for higher levels.
They are a top-tier U SPORTS prospect, low-tier D1 FCS, and high-tier D2 candidate.
SCORE:70-74

SOLID LEVEL PROSPECT:
PROJECTION: U SPORTS, D2.
This player possesses solid athletic and skill traits, making them a quality U SPORTS prospect and a D2-worthy player.
Players rated 64+ are considered quality recruits at the U SPORTS and D2 levels.
SCORE:58-69

SATISFACTORY LEVEL PROSPECT:
PROJECTION: U SPORTS, D3, CUFL, JUCO. This player either possesses developable physical traits or lacks ideal
measurables but is a solid football player. Either way, they are long-term projects with potential upside and are priority CUFL recruits.
SCORE:50-57

POOR LEVEL PROSPECT:
PROJECTION: CJFL/JUCO CAMP INVITE. This player lacks a strong distinct trait and is underwhelming. They struggle against their
current level of competition and fail to dominate. They're worth a camp invite at the CJFL and JUCO levels.
SCORE: 0-49
